Getting Started with HomeVue

Installation Guide

Follow these steps to install HomeVue on your device:

  1. Visit the App Store
  2. Search for "HomeVue AR Interior Design"
  3. Tap "Install" or "Get" to download the application
  4. Once installed, open the app and follow the on-screen setup instructions
  5. Grant necessary permissions for camera and storage access

Tip: For optimal performance, ensure your device has at least 2GB of RAM and runs iOS 13+ or Android 9.0+.

Room Scanning Tutorial

To get the most accurate results when scanning your room:

  • Ensure good lighting conditions in the room
  • Remove small obstacles from the floor
  • Start in one corner and slowly move around the perimeter
  • Hold your device at waist height for best results
  • Make sure to capture all walls, floors, and major furniture

Using the Design Tools

HomeVue offers powerful design tools to transform your space:

  • Furniture Catalog: Browse thousands of 3D furniture models
  • Drag & Place: Simply drag items into your room view
  • Rotate: Use pinch and twist gestures to adjust items
  • Save Designs: Create multiple design variations for comparison

Troubleshooting Common Issues

AR Not Working Properly

If you're experiencing issues with AR functionality:

  1. Ensure you have adequate lighting in the room
  2. Check that your device supports AR
  3. Restart the app and try scanning again
  4. Make sure your camera lens is clean and unobstructed
  5. Update to the latest version of HomeVue
  6. Try scanning a room with more distinct features and textures

Battery Drain Issues

AR applications like HomeVue can be resource-intensive. To minimize battery drain:

  • Close other apps running in the background
  • Reduce screen brightness when using the app
  • Use the "Low Power Mode" in settings for longer scanning sessions
  • Save your progress frequently and close the app when not in use
  • Consider using a power bank for extended design sessions

App Performance Issues

If you notice lag or crashes:

  1. Clear the app cache (Settings > Apps > HomeVue > Storage > Clear Cache)
  2. Restart your device
  3. Ensure you have at least 1GB of free storage space
  4. Update to the latest version of the app
  5. For persistent issues, try uninstalling and reinstalling the app

Note: Reinstalling will delete local data unless you've backed up to the cloud with an account.
